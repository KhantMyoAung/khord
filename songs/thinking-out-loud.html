🎯 Performance Tips

  • The whole song only uses 5 buttons: A, S, F, G, H
  • The verse is just A → F → G repeated (D → G → A)
  • Try holding ↓ (joystick down / arrow down) to add Sus4 for a dreamy sound
  • Use → (joystick right) on the Em (S key) for a jazzy Em7
  • Set to Strum mode for a more guitar-like feel
  • Try recording the verse pattern in the Looper then play the chorus live on top!
